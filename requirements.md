# Yeu cau he thong Quan ly Nha hang

## Yeu cau chuc nang

**Quan ly tai khoan**
- Dang ky, dang nhap, phan quyen (Admin, Nhan vien, Khach hang)
- Quen mat khau, xac thuc email (OTP)
- Admin quan ly tai khoan nhan vien (them/sua/xoa)

**Quan ly thuc don**
- Them/sua/xoa danh muc va mon an
- Hien thi danh sach mon theo danh muc co hinh anh, gia

**Quan ly don hang**
- Tao don hang (chon ban, chon mon)
- Them/bot mon, cap nhat trang thai
- Tinh tong tien tu dong
- Cap nhat real-time (SignalR)

**Quan ly ban an**
- Them/sua/xoa ban (ten, suc chua, vi tri)
- So do ban truc quan, cap nhat real-time
- Chuyen trang thai ban (trong, co khach, dat truoc)

**Quan ly khach hang**
- Luu thong tin va lich su khach hang
- Tim kiem khach hang

**Dat ban truoc**
- Dat ban theo ngay gio, chon ban trong
- Xac nhan va thong bao real-time

**Thong ke**
- Doanh thu, so don, mon ban chay
- Loc theo ngay/tuan/thang

**Goi y AI**
- Goi y mon an dua tren lich su
- Goi y xep ban

## Yeu cau phi chuc nang

**Hieu nang**: API phan hoi nhanh (< 500ms), real-time < 1s, chiu duoc 100+ nguoi dung dong thoi.

**Bao mat**: Mat khau ma hoa BCrypt, xac thuc JWT, phan quyen theo role, bao ve API co ban.

**Tin cay**: Sao luu du lieu hang ngay, he thong 24/7, xu ly loi co van ban ro rang.

**Mo rong**: Co the them may chu khi can, thiet ke de them nha hang moi.

**Su dung**: Giao dien hien dai, tuong thich dien thoai, thao tac nhanh gon.

**Tich hop**: Ket noi duoc DeepSeek API, email service, dinh dang JSON thong nhat.
